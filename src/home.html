Welcome To Sentimental Analysis Website

Sentiment Analysis is the process of ‘computationally’ determining whether a piece of writing is positive, negative or neutral.
Sentiment analysis, also referred to as opinion mining or emotion extraction is the classification of emotions within a textual data.
Opinion mining technique has been widely used in order to determine the sentiments, emotions within a particular textual data.
Twitter is a social media platform that has been mostly used by people to express emotions for particular events.
This Website collected tweets for a number of events from searched hashtag, analyzed them using a Machine Learning algorithms Naïve Bayes and finally generate graph for that sentimental analysis of searched hashtag tweets.

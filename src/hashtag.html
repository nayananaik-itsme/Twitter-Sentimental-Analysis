Twitter Hashtag Sentimental Analysis

Enter A Any Twitter Hashtag



On Twitter, adding a “#” to the beginning of an unbroken word or phrase creates a hashtag.

When use a hashtag in a Tweet, it becomes linked to all of the other Tweets that include it.

Including a hashtag gives a Tweet context and can give a conversation longevity.

Clicking or tapping on a hashtagged word in any message shows you other Tweets that include that hashtag.

Cannot add spaces or punctuation in a hashtag, or it will not work properly.
